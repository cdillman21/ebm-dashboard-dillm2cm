Comprehensive Evaluation Framework

1. Outcome Evaluation (Did it work?)

  • Primary Outcome: Early-career retention rate (measured annually)
  • Target: Increase from 60% baseline to 75%+ by end of Year 2
  • Data Source: HRIS system - automated retention tracking by tenure cohort
  • Analysis: Compare pilot group vs. control group + before-after analysis

2. Process Evaluation (Is it being implemented as designed?)

  • Training Completion: 90%+ of managers complete all 40 hours of training
  • Skill Application: 80%+ of managers demonstrate competencies in observation assessments
  • 1-on-1 Frequency: 90%+ of employees receive monthly 1-on-1 meetings
  • Compensation Alignment: 95%+ of salaries within market range (45th-55th percentile)

3. Impact Evaluation (What else changed?)

  • Employee Engagement: +20 point increase on annual survey (baseline 65 â†’ target 85)
  • Manager Effectiveness: +30 point improvement in direct report ratings
  • Productivity: +12% output per employee (measured by revenue per FTE)
  • Cost Savings: $600K annual reduction in turnover costs
  • Unintended Consequences: Monitor for equity concerns, manager burnout, budget overruns

Mixed-Methods Data Collection

  • Quantitative: Quarterly surveys (engagement, satisfaction, manager quality), HRIS retention data, performance metrics, compensation analysis
  • Qualitative: Manager interviews (quarterly), employee focus groups (bi-annual), exit interviews (ongoing), observation of 1-on-1 meetings
  • Document Review: Training completion records, development plan documentation, performance review quality
  • Comparative Analysis: Pilot vs. control group, Year-over-year trends, industry benchmarks
